Data Communication

Data communication is the process of transferring data or infromation in different forms like text, images, sound, video etc. from sender to receiver.

Examples:

1. Emails
2. SMS
3. Multimedia Message Service
4. Phone calls
5. Server-client communication
6. Video conference

Components

There are six components of data communication. They are message, sender, receiver, communication channel, encoder and decoder.
